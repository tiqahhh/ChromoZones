# 🧬 Zooming Meiosis - Interactive Learning App

An interactive, comprehensive learning platform designed to help secondary students understand meiosis I and meiosis II through 3D visualizations, detailed explanations, comparison tables, and engaging exercises.

## 📋 Features

### 1. **Home Page (index.html)**
- Overview of the app and its features
- Quick reference information about meiosis
- Navigation to all sections
- Visual cards explaining key learning areas

### 2. **3D Intro & Video (intro.html)**
- **Interactive 3D Visualization**: 
  - Visual models of all meiosis stages (Prophase I, Metaphase I, Anaphase I, Prophase II, Metaphase II, Anaphase II)
  - Click and drag to rotate the cell
  - Scroll to zoom in/out
  - Stage buttons for easy navigation
  - Color-coded chromosomes and spindle fibers

- **Video Introduction**: 
  - Embedded YouTube video explaining the complete meiosis process
  - Detailed stage-by-stage explanations
  - Key concepts for each phase

- **Detailed Explanations**:
  - Comprehensive breakdown of Meiosis I (Reduction Division)
  - Comprehensive breakdown of Meiosis II (Equational Division)
  - Sources of genetic variation (crossing over and independent assortment)

### 3. **Comparison Tables (comparison.html)**
- **Meiosis I vs Meiosis II**: Side-by-side comparison table
- **Meiosis vs Mitosis**: Comprehensive comparison table covering:
  - Purpose
  - Number of divisions
  - Daughter cells produced
  - Chromosome changes
  - Genetic variation
  - Key differences

- **Chromosomal Events Summary**:
  - Synapsis & Tetrad
  - Crossing Over (Recombination)
  - Independent Assortment
  - Chiasmata

- **DNA Content Changes**: Visual representations of DNA changes during meiosis

### 4. **Flashcards & Quiz (exercise.html)**

#### Flashcards:
- **Basic Concepts**: What is meiosis, diploid/haploid, cell division
- **Stages**: Details about each phase of meiosis
- **Processes**: Synapsis, crossing over, independent assortment
- **Comparisons**: Meiosis vs mitosis differences

Click on cards to flip and reveal answers!

#### Interactive Quiz:
- **10 Multiple-choice questions** covering all major concepts
- Progress tracking
- Instant feedback on correct/incorrect answers
- Score calculation and personalized message
- Ability to retake the quiz

#### Key Terms Dictionary:
- 10 essential meiosis terms defined
- Clear, student-friendly explanations

## 🚀 How to Use

### Getting Started:
1. Open `index.html` in a web browser
2. Navigate using the menu bar at the top
3. Start with the 3D Intro & Video to get a visual understanding

### For Learning:
1. **Page 1 (Home)**: Read the overview to understand what you'll learn
2. **Page 2 (3D Intro & Video)**: 
   - Watch the video
   - Interact with the 3D visualizations by dragging and zooming
   - Read stage explanations
3. **Page 3 (Comparisons)**: 
   - Study the comparison tables
   - Understand differences between related processes
4. **Page 4 (Exercises)**: 
   - Use flashcards to memorize key terms
   - Take the quiz to test your knowledge

### For Practice:
- Use flashcards regularly for quick review
- Retake the quiz until you score 80% or above
- Refer back to comparison tables when needed

## 📖 Content Covered

### Meiosis I (Reduction Division):
- Prophase I: Synapsis, crossing over, nuclear envelope breakdown
- Metaphase I: Bivalent alignment
- Anaphase I: Homolog separation
- Telophase I: Cell division

### Meiosis II (Equational Division):
- Prophase II: Chromosome condensation
- Metaphase II: Chromosome alignment
- Anaphase II: Sister chromatid separation
- Telophase II: Final cell division

### Key Concepts:
- Diploid vs Haploid cells
- Homologous chromosomes
- Sister chromatids
- Synapsis and tetrads
- Crossing over (recombination)
- Independent assortment
- Genetic variation
- Gamete formation
